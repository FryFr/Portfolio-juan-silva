## Summary

In-person talk at ManageEngine's Partner Training & User Conference, held at the Sheraton Hotel in Bogotá in October 2024. I presented best practices for ServiceDesk Plus implementation in regional organizations, covering ITSM workflow configuration, ticket resolution automation, and strategies to maximize adoption by support teams.

## Topics covered

- **ITIL-based ITSM configuration**: how to map an organization's real processes to ServiceDesk Plus capabilities without over-engineering
- **Workflow automation**: business rules, triggers, and workflows that reduce the manual burden on support teams
- **Adoption strategies**: how to get teams to actually use the tool, beyond technical configuration
- **ManageEngine ecosystem integration**: how ServiceDesk Plus connects with other ITSM/ITOM suite tools

## Context

ManageEngine's Partner Training event brings together consultants, partners, and users from across Latin America to share technical knowledge and best practices about ManageEngine suite products. As a Technical Consultant, my role was to transfer field experience from real implementations to attendees.
