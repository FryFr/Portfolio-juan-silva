## Summary

In-person talk at the second edition of ManageEngine's Partner Training & User Conference at the Sheraton Hotel in Bogotá, April 2025. I presented advances in intelligent automation applied to ITSM, including the generative AI integration with ServiceDesk Plus that I had developed as a Technical Consultant.

## Topics covered

- **RAG applied to ITSM**: how to use Retrieval-Augmented Generation so the system suggests resolutions based on actual documentation and previous tickets
- **LLM integration with ServiceDesk Plus**: architecture of the system connecting web scraping, vector databases, and GPT-4 for automatic ticket resolution
- **Zoho Circuits as orchestrator**: how to use Zoho's automation platform to connect the AI backend with the ITSM product without modifying its core
- **Production lessons learned**: what worked, what didn't, and how the solution evolved after months in real use

## Context

This was the second time speaking at the ManageEngine event. Unlike the first edition (focused on implementation and ITIL), this talk centered on the intersection of AI and ITSM — a topic that emerged directly from the automation project I developed for the division.
