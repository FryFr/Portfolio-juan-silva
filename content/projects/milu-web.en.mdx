## The project

Milu is a premium Colombian shampoo bar brand. Not artisanal, not indie — premium in every sense. That framing drove every design decision and every line of code: the experience has to match the product.

![Milu logo — cat sleeping with moon](/images/projects/milu-logo.jpeg)

The project covers the full design and development of the website and online store. The product catalog is served from Supabase, with per-image lazy loading and well-defined error boundaries so no network hiccup can break the shopping experience. Checkout integrates MercadoPago with payment state handling, redirects, and order confirmations.

## Visual identity

Milu's identity is sophisticated by design. The cream and gold palette isn't decorative — it communicates accessible luxury, warmth, and care. Every color has a clear editorial role.

![Milu color palette](/images/projects/milu-colors.png)

The typography pairs two families with distinct roles: Cormorant Garamond for editorial headlines, with its elegant serifs and fashion-magazine rhythm; and Outfit for body text and UI, clean and modern. The tension between the two is exactly what gives the design its character.

![Typography system: Cormorant Garamond + Outfit](/images/projects/milu-typography.png)

## Miluna — the brand mascot

One of the most distinctive brand assets is Miluna, the illustrated mascot. She humanizes the e-commerce experience and turns content pages like About or Sustainability into something with genuine personality.

![Miluna — illustrated mascot for the Milu brand](/images/projects/milu-miluna.png)

## Stack and technical decisions

- **React 19 + Vite** — no framework overhead, optimized bundle, instant HMR in development.
- **TypeScript** — clear contracts between components and the data layer.
- **Supabase** — product catalog as the source of truth, with typed queries.
- **MercadoPago** — payment integration built for the Latin American market.
- **SEO** — dynamic meta tags per product, Open Graph, structured data.
- **Accessibility** — visible focus, ARIA roles, contrast validated across the full palette.

Content pages (About, FAQ, Ingredients, Sustainability, Contact) are static but share the same design system, ensuring visual consistency throughout the site.
