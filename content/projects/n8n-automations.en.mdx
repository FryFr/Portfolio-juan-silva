## The problem

Dynapro operates with multiple business tools: Odoo for ERP, Xero for accounting, HubSpot for CRM, CONTPAQi for fiscal invoicing, and more. Each system lived in isolation — information was duplicated manually, processes depended on human intervention, and synchronization errors caused time losses and inconsistent data.

There was no integration layer connecting these tools intelligently. Sales, operations, and finance teams worked with outdated or incomplete information.

## The solution

I designed and implemented an enterprise automation architecture using N8N as the central orchestrator. Each flow connects two or more tools in Dynapro's ecosystem to eliminate manual processes and guarantee real-time data consistency.

```
                    ┌─────────────────────────────────┐
                    │         N8N Orchestrator         │
                    │  (webhooks · schedulers · JS)    │
                    └────┬────────┬────────┬────────┬──┘
                         │        │        │        │
              ┌──────────┘  ┌─────┘  ┌────┘  ┌─────┘
              ▼             ▼        ▼        ▼
        ┌──────────┐  ┌──────────┐  ┌────────┐  ┌──────────┐
        │   Odoo   │  │   Xero   │  │HubSpot │  │CONTPAQi  │
        │  (ERP)   │  │(Accounting)│ │ (CRM)  │  │(Invoicing)│
        └──────────┘  └──────────┘  └────────┘  └──────────┘
              │             │            │
              └─────────────┴────────────┘
                     bidirectional sync
```

**Data synchronization**: Bidirectional flows between Odoo, Xero, and HubSpot that keep contacts, products, and transactions synchronized automatically.

**Lead management**: When a lead enters through HubSpot, it's automatically created in Odoo, assigned to the corresponding salesperson, and the team is notified.

**Purchase order automation**: From creation in Odoo to invoicing in CONTPAQi, the complete flow executes without manual intervention.

**Commissions and reports**: Automatic commission calculation based on closed sales, with report generation and team notifications.

**Operational playbooks**: Flows that execute predefined action sequences for ticket management, shipment tracking, and customer communication.

## Stack and decisions

**N8N** as orchestrator for its flexibility, self-hosting capability, and ability to integrate REST APIs, webhooks, and custom logic with JavaScript — without depending on Zapier or Make and their pricing limitations.

**Event-driven architecture**: Flows are triggered by webhooks, email triggers (Gmail), or schedulers, ensuring real-time execution without unnecessary polling.

**Custom JavaScript nodes**: For complex data transformations between systems with different schemas — field normalization, cross-ID mapping, and integrity validation.

## Impact

- Elimination of manual synchronization processes across 5+ tools
- Reduction of human errors in data entry and invoicing
- Centralized visibility of lead, order, and shipment status
- Scalability: each new flow builds on the existing infrastructure without reconfiguration
